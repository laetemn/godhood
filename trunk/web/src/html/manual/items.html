Items

Every adventurer should have a good understanding of the items in his possession, and how to use them.

Picking Up

Improbable as it sounds, you will occasionally find an object lying around on the ground. If fortune does bless you in this fashion you can pick it up by standing above it and selecting Pick Up from the action menu.

Dropping

Dropping an item is also easy, although given the notorious avarice of adventurers, far less common. To drop an item, select the drop action associated with that item from the action menu. For example, if you wanted to drop a potion of healing, you would select the action labeled Drop - potion of healing, you should notice a potion of healing sitting at your feet.

Using

Every item in Godhood has a use of some description, all the actions available from an item in your possession will be post-fixed with the items name. For example, if I had a potion of healing, I could do two things with it, specifically
  • Drop - potion of healing
  • Drink - potion of healing
I may have many potions of healing, however, since I can only drink or drop one at a time, the respective options will only appear once.
Fixed Items
In your travels, you may come across items that you can't pick up, but are still useful to you. By far the most common example of this type of item, is the archway used to move to another room. To use a fixed item, simply stand on top of it and any additional actions that that item allows will appear in the action menu.

Effects

Some items confer effects onto their owner, typically the effect will be obvious based on the item. For example, a suit of armour will make make you harder to hit, boots of speed will make you faster, and gauntlets of strength will make you stronger.
