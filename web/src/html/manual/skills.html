Skills

Chat

Talks to an adjacent monster, the monster may tell you something useful, or it may ignore you entirely.

Defend

While defending, your attack power is added to your defence power thus reducing the chance of being hit. The drawback is that you will not attack monsters that move into range. If you are struck while defending then you immediately stop, and subsequent attacks have a normal probability of hitting. This action ends the current turn.

End Turn

Ends the current turn.

Move

Enters move mode, see the movement section for more information.

Pickpocket

Steals an item from the target monster. The thief specialises in this action.

Pray

Prays to your characters god, the effect of this action is completely at your gods discretion. The cleric has a close enough relationship with his god to be able to do this.

Recruit

Recruits a friendly monster to your cause, this is a time consuming process and only works on monsters who are weaker than you are. The cleric and the fighter pride themselves on their people skills, hence can perform this action.

Sleeping

Sometimes you'll be caught napping, unfortunately sleeping for a few seconds on the battlefield has absolutely no benefits and is quite dangerous. Being hit by another monster will wake you up, but you'll also take double damage for your troubles.

Turn Undead

The cleric hates the undead, they represent the antithesis of everything he stands for, plus they kind of freak him out. Luckily for him, his piety gives him a certain degree of power over these abominations.

Unlock

You can unlock doors with this action, this is handy if you want to walk through them. The thief can unlock doors without any special equipment.
