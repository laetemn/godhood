Introduction

Welcome to Godhood - The Eye of Ascension, a fantasy role-playing game for wireless devices.

Background

In the world of Godhood the gods rule the earth. Worship and belief can, given enough of it, turn anyone or anything into a god (or a devil). In principal, it's the ultimate marriage of democracy and religion, the god you get is the god you want. In reality, any group of misguided fools can suddenly open the doors to godhood.

The Story so Far

The cult of Baromophet is one of the most beguiling, and hence dangerous, in the known world. Followers are encouraged to partake heavily of narcotics, and to indulge in wanton acts of carnality. The Baromophet cult enjoyed a brief, but significant, rise to power over one thousand years ago. During this time Baromophet himself was said to walk the earth, leaving a moral vacuum in his wake. Many were sent against him, however those few that came back were a sick parody of their original selves. If anything, the survivors were more of a blow to the morale of the waning resistance than those who disappeared.

In the end it was Baromophets own greed that destroyed him. Seeking out more power, he obtained the fabled Eye of Ascension, and attempted to ascend to the Mantle of the Gods. However the Eye consumed Baromophet instead, or at least, that's the theory as both the Eye and Baromophet disappeared. The cult members, robbed of their dark god, soon turned on eachother. The surviving cult members were eventually driven underground, to watch, and wait.

Now, a millenia later, the cult of Baromophet stirs again. The Eye of Ascension has been located in a small temple on the outskirts of civilisation. You have been sent to defend it at all costs. However as you approach your destination you notice that all is not as it should be...

Finishing the Game

The ultimate goal of the game is to locate the Eye of Ascension and return it to the level in which you start the game.
