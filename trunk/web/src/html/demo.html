Welcome to the demo game. This release is designed to showcase some of Godhood - The Eye of Ascensions capabilities, I hope you enjoy it.

In hindsight it over it was probably a mistake to host the wedding at Sacrifice Peak in the Dragonmount ranges. All the guests had arrived, the weather was perfect, Lydias parents had even called a truce for the occasion. During the exchanging of vows, as if on cue, a blast of fire incinerated the minister. In the stunned silence that followed, a voice boomed out "It is I, Alderstat. You'll all pay for this insult!".

They're all dead now, eaten by Alderstat. Once you regained conciousness, all you think of was revenge, in a way it was all you could do to take your mind off the pain. For days you've run towards the smoking peak that marks Alderstats home, and now you're here. Taking a last, deep breath of fresh air you step into the mouth of the cavern...
