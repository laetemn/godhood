Magic

Background

Magic in Godhood is poorly understood at best. Even the most talented wizards in the realm have been known to admit that they have very little idea what they are doing. Understanding of magic is greatly hampered by the fact that, while the caster can read and learn a spell at any time, he has no memory of the spell once he has cast it. Wizards involved in rituals that span hours, or even days, have reported finding themselves with no memory where they are, or how they got there, and more importantly, no idea how to get back.

The origins of magic are tied to the gods, who dispense their knowledge of magic to mortals as they see fit. Typically a spell or item has fairly unilateral applications (like really hurting someone), and the vessel has only a very limited understanding of its workings anyway.

It is possible to capture a memorized spell on paper with enough time, effort, and motivation. The gods, as a group, and with some justification, view this process as theft. Depending on the circumstances, their retribution can be quite swift and bloody. In spite of this, there exists a thriving and well organised black market in magic spells and magic items, the bread and butter of wizardry.

Casting Spells

To cast a spell, simply select the spell from the action list. Some spells will enter the attack interface, at this point it is identical to attacking someone, as described in the combat section. Others will work instantaneously.

Learning Spells

To learn a spell, you'll need to have a spellbook, you'll also need to be trained in reading magic (like the magician). Alternately you can try praying to your god who, if he/she is feeling in a good mood, may grant you a spell or two.

Common Spells

The following is a list of generic spells.
Sleep
Causes the target to fall into a magical slumber.
Magic Missile
The stock standard attack spell in a wizards arsenal, a favorite as it packs a punch and it never fails to hit the target.
Knock
A handy spell for opening locked doors.
Summoning
Summoning spells call forth a creature of some description to do the casters bidding.
